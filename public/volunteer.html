This project is designed for potential dog owners to adopt from a non-profit dog rescue called, “Freedom Tails Rescue”. The animal rescue was designed by a family friend of one of the team members of this project.Our motivations for creating a dog rescue website came from the hundreds of dogs in Maricopa County that need loving homes. Many come from county shelters, neglectful situations, abandonment situations and owner surrenders. We believe in giving these dogs a chance at the best life possible.
