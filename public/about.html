Our motivations for creating a dog rescue website came from the hundreds of dogs in Maricopa County that need loving homes. Many come from county shelters, neglectful situations, abandonment situations and owner surrenders. We believe in giving these dogs a chance at the best life possible.






Freedom Tails Animal Rescue is a 501(c) 3 non-profit organization based in Maricopa County, Arizona
