Giving up a best friend is never easy...

When considering to surrender your pets, we would like to offer the following options before deciding that rehoming is best for you.

  • Ask family and friends to see if anyone is looking for a pet!
  • If its related to income, food pantries can often assist in obtaining pet food.
  • If its related to vet bills, talk to your vet and discuss what is extremely important and services that can wait.
  • If your pet isn't adjusting to their new forever home, think about obedience training.
  • If you've tried these and other methods and still find that you're unable to keep your furry friend, contact us at (480) 688-6663.
