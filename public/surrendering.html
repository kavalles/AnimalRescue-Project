When considering to surrender your pets, we would like to offer the following options before deciding that rehoming is best for you.

  1. Ask family and friends to see if anyone is looking for a pet!
  2. If its related to income, food pantries can often assist in obtaining pet food.
  3. If its related to vet bills, talk to your vet and discuss what is extremely important and services that can wait.
  4. If your pet isn't adjusting to their new forever home, think about obedience training.
If you've tried these and other methods and still find that you're unable to keep your furry friend, contact us at (480) 688-6663.
