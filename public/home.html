Welcome to Freedom Tails Rescue.

When you signup for an account, you can apply for adoption and volunteer. You can also support us by donating.

Freedom Tails is a non-profit organization based out of Maricopa County, Arizona dedicated to rescuing animals in need and placing them in loving foster homes.
Through adoption fees, donations and animal sponsorships we are able to help the homeless animals of Maricopa County until they find their forever home. Tragically, every year more than 2.7 million animals euthanized in the country. Our mission is to reduce this number one at a time, saving animal lives and improving human lives by providing them with companionship. Whether you're looking to volunteer, or just want to support us in other ways, welcome to Freedom Tails.
